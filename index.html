Built for people who want safer beauty care.

The Problem

Many beauty products contain harsh chemicals. They can harm the skin and hair.

People want safer solutions. But many so-called natural products do not work well.

This is a real need, especially for adults who want simple, natural care for hair loss and wrinkles.

The Solution

Homemade Remedies is a natural, safe, and effective line of products for hair loss and wrinkles.

Our products help adults who are concerned about hair thinning and signs of aging. The goal is stronger hair and smoother skin through natural care.

  • ✓
    The products are easy to use. Customers apply them and can see results in weeks.
  • ✓
    We prepare our remedies with natural ingredients and use eco-friendly packaging.
Who We Serve

Our target market is health-conscious women and men aged 25 to 27.

They need natural solutions for hair loss and wrinkles because they want safer beauty products.

Business Structure

Homemade Remedies will operate with 1 manager and 3 employees.

A lean and efficient team focused on producing, packaging, and delivering natural beauty products.

Market Proof

We surveyed 27 respondents and confirmed strong business potential.

The research showed that Homemade Remedies has strong potential among urban adults who are looking for natural beauty solutions.
