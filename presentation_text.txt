Describe  the target market’s need - Health-conscious women and men aged 25-27 need natural solutions for hair loss and wrinkles because they want safer  beauty. Business Description - Homemade Remedies for Hair Loss / Wrinkles is a natural, safe, and effective line of hair and skin care products that helps adults concerned with hair thinning and aging achieve stronger hair and smoother skin naturally.   - It’s very easy to use. The customer applies the product and sees results in weeks. - We prepare remedies using natural ingredients and package in eco-friendly containers. - “Homemade Remedies” will have 1 manager and 3 employees.

Marketing Market  Research - I surveyed 27 respondents and found that “Homemade Remedies” has a very promising business opportunity with urban adults looking for natural beauty solutions.   Evidence - I asked respondents: "Do you prefer natural remedies over chemical products?" The results showed that 80% answered yes, indicating strong demand.

Competitive Analysis - There  are 5 direct competitors with average price 60-100  somoni  per product. Indirect competitors include commercial hair oils and anti-wrinkle creams . Marketing  Mix - I will attract customers by offering natural products (hair oils, face masks, serums) at 50-90  somoni , selling online and in local stores, and promoting via social media.
